About Me

My name is Anna Claire Church. I am currently working on my Master's degree in Sociology at the University of Memphis with an expected graduation date of May 2019. Afterwards I will be pursuing further education at a Ph.D. granting university where I hope to be an active researcher of infant and maternal mortality disparities as well as the social aspects of breastfeeding. Thank you for visiting my website! Feel free to look around and do not hesitate to send me any questions you may have.
